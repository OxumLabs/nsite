What is Neit? 🔍

Neit is not just a language—it’s an open playground, a blank canvas for creative coders who dare to dream big. At its heart lies the NTune engine, a marvel of flexibility and power. This engine lets you, yes *you*, craft your own syntax at runtime, dynamically and without restrictions. Want to reshape the rules of coding? Go ahead! NTune turns you into the sculptor of your own language.

Crafting Your Own Syntax! 📜

Ever fantasized about creating a language all your own? Well, Neit makes that dream come true! It frees you from the shackles of traditional programming syntax. You’re not just a programmer—you’re the captain of your own ship, navigating uncharted coding waters.

Neit’s compilation process is lightning-fast—it’s like a sprinter who never tires. It compiles to highly optimized native assembly for Linux in the blink of an eye, and can also generate robust C-based binaries using Clang and LLD, which means it works flawlessly on both Windows and Linux. macOS? It's on the way, making its stylish entrance just a bit fashionably late.

Simplicity and Fun! ✨

Neit isn’t just about power, though. It’s about keeping things simple and fun. Say goodbye to hours of writing tedious boilerplate code! Neit strips away all that drudgery so you can focus on what really matters: the thrill of building something amazing. Whether you’re a veteran coder or a curious newcomer, Neit ensures that your journey is smooth, fast, and enjoyable, with no detours into frustration-ville.

Now, let’s talk about the language's playful personality—its error messages (pun intended!). Neit doesn’t throw grumpy, cryptic warnings at you. No, no, no. In Neit, error messages are more like friendly mentors, full of charm and wisdom.

They’re here to gently guide you with a pat on the back and, occasionally, a light-hearted joke. Forget the dreaded walls of red text—Neit’s errors give you a nudge in the right direction, often with a wink and a smile, and a little encouragement to keep you going.

The Magic of Neit-Distro-Lang!

Here’s where things get really interesting. With Neit’s *distro-lang* feature, you aren’t stuck coding in some rigid, predefined syntax. Imagine you’re handed a box of colorful crayons—Neit-distro-lang lets you use any color you want, or mix and match as you see fit!

Using user-defined grammar files, Neit can translate *your* custom syntax at compile time, seamlessly converting it into Neit’s native language. Whether you want a small tweak, like changing `if` to `when`, or go completely wild and invent your own syntax from scratch, it’s all possible.

Want to turn your code into an entirely new dialect? Sure thing! You can even mix and match grammars, creating a hybrid syntax that combines the best of your favorite languages. It’s not necessary to change *every* rule—sometimes small adjustments can make all the difference.

But the real magic is in the freedom to reinvent as much or as little as you like. Code like a pirate with your own treasure map, where every symbol has a secret meaning known only to you. With Neit-distro-lang, your programming language is what *you* make of it, and the NTune engine handles all the heavy lifting behind the scenes.

Limitless Creativity!

Neit isn’t here to limit you. It’s here to unleash your creativity and let you build your own coding experience from the ground up. The canvas is yours, the tools are sharp, and the potential is endless.

Neit may not come with a massive, bloated standard library—but that’s intentional. Why? Because the goal is to foster creativity. To encourage you to build only what you need and nothing more. You’re not handed a rigid set of tools; instead, Neit provides a blank slate, allowing you to craft the ultimate toolkit that fits your specific project.

It’s the essence of minimalism with a dash of magic.
