Neit Is Ready to Party on Your System!

Ready to install? Just grab the binary from the bins folder and throw it in /usr/local/bin/neit (or wherever your OS likes to keep things).

Neit loves compilers and will go through a checklist: zig? Cool. No? Okay, clang? Still no? Fine, gcc? If none of these are present, Neit throws a tantrum and refuses to compile. So, make sure you have at least one!
